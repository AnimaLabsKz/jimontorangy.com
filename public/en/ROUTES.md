# Local Routes

- `/` -> home
- `/walk_in_jimon/` -> About JIMON
- `/we_brands/` -> Our Brands
- `/product_category/` -> Planning and Structure
- `/product/` -> Products
- `/post_list/` -> News Center
- `/post_list?cid=1` -> redirects to `/post_list-cid-1/` (Company News)
- `/post_list?cid=4` -> redirects to `/post_list-cid-4/` (Health Topics)
- `/post_list?cid=5` -> redirects to `/post_list-cid-5/` (New Media Center)
- `/post_detail?id=119` -> redirects to `/post_detail-119/` (Article 119)
- `/post_detail?id=120` -> redirects to `/post_detail-120/` (Article 120)
- `/vip_center/` -> Member Area
- `/contact_us/` -> Contact Us
- `/law/` -> Legal Statement
