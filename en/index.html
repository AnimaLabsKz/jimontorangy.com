Areas of focus

Chinese Medicine Products
With four Chinese medicine brands each with over 100 years of history, more than a hundred drug approvals, 30 national patents, and 11 exclusive products, we have launched a series of products based on traditional Chinese medicine science to strive for a healthy life for all.
health management
Under the guidance of technological innovation in traditional Chinese medicine, the rise of the silver economy + overweight people + the number of people suffering from chronic diseases + the background of centralized purchasing. Six major product series of the big health brand: "medicines, health foods, tea drinks, special dietary drugs, medical equipment, maintenance and cosmetics"
Global perspective
Currently, we have established cooperative relationships with Singapore, South Korea, Japan, Australia, Canada, Indonesia, Malaysia, Slovakia and other countries. Maple Seed Oil has been sold in Singapore and Malaysia and has gained wide recognition and consumer reputation.
Scientific research strength
Established cooperation with 6 major scientific research institutions including Tianjin University of Traditional Chinese Medicine and China Academy of Chinese Medical Sciences, established the Hebei Traditional Chinese Medicine Industry Technology Research Institute, and reached strategic cooperation with Shanghai Super Laboratory in 2025.
social responsibility
A total of more than 50 million yuan has been donated in materials, and more than 10,000 acres of traditional Chinese medicine planting bases have been built to drive local economic development.
