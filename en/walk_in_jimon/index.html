The 33rd anniversary of the brand’s founding

JIMON was founded in 1993 and is headquartered in Anguo, Hebei Province, China's "Millennium Medicine Capital". It has 33 years of experience in the traditional Chinese medicine industry and nearly 2,000 employees.

JIMON has built two core advantageous sectors: traditional Chinese medicine industry and general health industry. In the pharmaceutical field, the group has integrated four century-old brands, including Baoding Traditional Chinese Medicine, Anyao Group, Yuhontang Holding Group and Yishoutang, to form a complete industrial chain integrating medicinal planting, intensive processing, manufacturing and terminal sales. It has 300 drug approval numbers, 56 national patents, 11 exclusive products, and is equipped with high-standard modern production lines. Its products are not only sold well in the national market, but also exported to Singapore, Canada and other countries and regions, steadily promoting the international layout of traditional Chinese medicine and general health.

TMWS industrial service concept

sIndustrial Service Philosophy

The comprehensive health industry of JIMON is based on the innovative thinking of traditional Chinese medicine science and technology, based on the industrial service concept of T (traditional TRADITION) + M (modern MODERN) + W (wisdom WISDOM) + S (scientific SCIENCE), to provide high-quality life solutions for the contemporary population. Moving toward "newness" and pursuing "quality", JIMON's comprehensive health system integrates and develops in multiple industries.

The traditional Chinese medicine intelligent chemical factory activates new vitality in the industry. The group has successively built a R&D and production base for JIMONshan power functional foods, a R&D and production base for JIMONjinshan Ant dietary products, and a R&D and production base for JIMON Snow Orchid cosmetics, forming a professional and large-scale industrial layout.

In the field of industrial innovation, JIMON Health has created a private e-commerce innovation ecological platform "JIMON Selection" and JIMON Health Management Center to continue to strengthen its own industrial advantages. The Group promotes the deep integration of traditional Chinese medicine and modern technology, uses digital intelligence to empower high-quality development of the platform, closely integrates brand concepts with users' health needs, continuously innovates traditional Chinese medicine health communication methods, reaches every user with precise health services, and works hand in hand with everyone to move forward and achieve their dreams.

Scientific research-based, experimental evidence

scientific research

As a high-tech enterprise, we integrate core capabilities such as scientific research and co-creation, formula research and development, product development, and flexible supply chain management to provide customers with full-chain, integrated comprehensive service solutions.

Technology JIMON, refined quality, JIMON’s global health and smart ecological strategy

From the integration and innovation of global health smart ecology and C-side thinking to the "intestinal-X axis" health management system, to the establishment of a global "six-dimensional selection" supply chain; from linking authoritative scientific research forces to realizing integrated management and control of industry, academia, research and marketing, JIMON has been steadily moving forward on the road of upgrading the traditional Chinese medicine industry with the dual-wheel drive of R&D and supply chain, leaving a solid and clear footprint. This move not only brings more accurate and more secure health protection to the majority of users, but also creates a more stable and long-term development space for partners, truly realizing value symbiosis and win-win cooperation with every user and every partner.​

JIMON's global health and smart ecological strategy integrates the group's resources and advantages in R&D, production, sales, services and other fields to build a healthy and smart ecosystem covering the entire population, the entire life cycle, and the entire industry chain. Through the upgrade and transformation of the JIMON brand, we see not only the growth and expansion of a traditional Chinese medicine company, but also the inheritance and rebirth of a traditional Chinese medicine culture. With reverence for life, persistence in quality, and pursuit of innovation, JIMON keeps walking on the road of protecting national health, pursuing the light, and using practical actions to contribute the power of "golden quality" to promote the modernization of traditional Chinese medicine and protect national health.
